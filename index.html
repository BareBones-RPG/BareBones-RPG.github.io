BareBones TTRPG

A fast-paced tabletop role-playing game using six-sided dice.

BareBones RPG is a fast-paced game for long or short sessions. Easy to learn and fun to play!

Game Mechanics

BareBones RPG, players possess attributes, each associated with a specific number of six-sided dice for actions related to that attribute. Rolling a 5 on a die is a soft success, while a roll of 6 is a critical success. Any result below 4 is a soft failure, and a roll of 1 is a fumble, potentially leading to harmful consequences. However, if a player holds their action for a round or wants to react to an enemy's action, the success threshold is reduced to 4, instead of the usual 5-6.

What You Need...

  • 3 to 6 players, with one acting as the Game Master, Facilitator, or Referee.
  • Pencil
  • Index Cards or Character Sheet
  • At least 3 to 6 six-sided dice
  • Tokens or Mini-figures to represent playable characters known as PC
  • Your imagination

Attributes

Barebones uses these attributes whenever a player do a test that

  • AGILITY = Defend, balance, swim, flee
  • INSIGHT = Casting spells, perceive, aim, charm, smarts
  • MIGHT = Crush, lift, strike, grapple
  • WILLPOWER = Resist fear, using relics/magic weapons, rituals
